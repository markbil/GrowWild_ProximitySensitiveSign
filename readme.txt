Grow Wild: An Interactive, Proximity Sensitive Sign

A friend of mine had one of his moss graffiti signs exhibited and asked me to pimp it up a bit. We used an RGB LED strip connected to an Arduino and an ultrasonic distance sensor to make the sign glow more or less depending on how close a spectator comes.
